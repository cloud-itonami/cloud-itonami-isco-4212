# Business Model: Independent Gaming Table & Wagering Operations Practice

## Classification

- Repository: `cloud-itonami-isco-4212`
- ISCO-08: `4212`
- Occupation: Bookmakers, Croupiers and Related Gaming Workers
- Social impact: consumer-protection, responsible-gaming, local-jobs

## Customer

- licensed gaming venues
- private gaming events
- patrons

## Offer

- table operation
- wager settlement
- patron age/identity verification
- compliance reporting

## Revenue

- table operation fee
- per-event staffing fee
- compliance reporting package

## Trust Controls

- no payout above the table's registered limit without governor gate
- age/identity verification required before any wager is accepted
- wager and payout records are auditable, not editable
